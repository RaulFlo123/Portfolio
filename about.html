About Me

My name is Raul Flores and I am a high school student passionate about technology, programming, and web design. I enjoy building interactive websites and learning new tools such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, and animation libraries like Anime.js. Some of my projects include posters, interactive quizzes, wireframes, and web applications such as an Animal Shelter Locator and a restaurant website. My goal is to continue developing my programming skills and pursue a career in technology or software engineering.
